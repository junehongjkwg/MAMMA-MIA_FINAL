The Method · Editor's Note

How we
build
brands.

01

Listen, then question.

Every brand we touch starts with the same posture — we sit down, we listen, then we politely question everything the founder thinks they know about their market. The best identities are built from uncomfortable truths.

02

Position before pixel.

Strategy lives upstream of design. We map the competitive landscape, locate the gap nobody else has the courage to fill, and only then do we let the typography decisions begin.

03

Design the system, not the asset.

A logo is one frame. A brand is the entire film. We design colour, type, voice, and motion as a single system — so every menu, post, and napkin recognises itself in the family.

04

Hand it over with care.

A brand deck without context is a museum piece. We deliver guidelines, working files, and live training — so your team can carry the identity confidently into rooms we'll never enter.
